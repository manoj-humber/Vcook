Little Story

It all started back in 2019 when two GTU graduates, Kuldip Patel and Charmi patel decided they wanted to make life easier by changing the way world eats - all with just a tap! With their idea of ’hyperlocal food delivery’, all they needed was the tech to power it and were introduced to Manoj Kumar, who brought this vision to life with the website. And with this, VCook was launched as a food ordering & delivery platform.

With love and support from consumers, VCook expanded far and wide, first through the entire city of Bengaluru and then across the entire country. As they say, the rest is history.

Today, VCook is the leading food ordering and delivery platform in India. The innovative technology, large and nimble delivery service, and exceptional consumer focus at VCook enabled a host of benefits that includes lightning fast deliveries, live order tracking and no restrictions on order amount, all while having the pleasure of enjoying your favourite meal wherever you'd like it.
